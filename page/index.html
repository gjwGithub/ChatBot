About US

Welcome to the WaitriX Online Management System

Our web-based chatbot, Waitrix, is concerned about your customers’ restaurant experience. From table reservation, food ordering, dish recommendation to dispute handling, Waitrix is dedicated to support customers with their needs in a simple way.

Waitrix is easy to use. Within minutes of usage, Waitrix can help customers get ready with their upcoming service. Unlike any other tedious app that confuses your customers by dumping tons of choices to them, Waitrix will list choices by analyzing their preferences and previous chat history.

Waitrix can help your business grow. It can provide a comfortable place for customers to make their complaints or suggestions anonymously. With these constructive feedback and our monthly generated selling reports at hand, a great future for your restaurant is within reach.

Waitrix is consisted of two parts: a backend interface wherein you can enter your restaurant’s menu, and a frontend web page wherein customers can chat with our bot. The backend is fast to deploy and easy to navigate, the frontend is intuitive and without redundant information.
